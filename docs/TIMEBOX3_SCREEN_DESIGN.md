## 5.3.4 Iteration for Screen Design

### Iteration 1

Users said that the timetable page is difficult to read when many subjects appear in the same week. So, a week-grid layout is used with clearer time slots, colour labels per course/subject, and hover details for each session.

### Iteration 2

Users said that recording attendance takes too long and it is easy to miss students. So, the attendance form layout is improved with a searchable student list, bulk “Mark all Present”, and clear counters (present/absent/total).

### Iteration 3

Users said that announcements requiring acknowledgement were not obvious, causing missed acknowledgements. So, acknowledgement-required announcements are highlighted with a badge and a clear action button on the announcement detail.

### Iteration 4

Users said that contact and feedback inbox pages were hard to manage due to large numbers of messages. So, search, filters, unread badges, and pagination controls were improved for faster scanning and processing.
