## 5.2.4 Iteration for Screen Design

### Iteration 1 – Grade Visibility & Filters

Users report that the My Grades Page does not make it easy to see which grades are approved versus pending or rejected. The initial design only shows raw scores without clear status indicators. The layout is updated to include **status badges (Pending, Approved, Rejected)**, a **semester filter**, and grouping by **Course** and **Subject** for better clarity.

### Iteration 2 – Fee Overview & Overdue Highlighting

Students say that the My Fees Page makes it difficult to identify overdue fees and understand their overall fee status. The design is updated to add **status chips**, an **overdue highlight** (e.g. red badge for overdue), and a **summary panel** showing total fees, paid amount, pending amount, and overdue count.

### Iteration 3 – Assignment Submission Clarity

Students mention that the Assignment Detail & Submit Page does not clearly indicate the allowed file types, maximum file size, or whether resubmission is allowed. The screen is updated to show **allowed formats**, **max size**, **due date and time**, and a clear **submission status** (Not Submitted / Submitted / Graded), with helper text explaining resubmission behaviour.

### Iteration 4 – Teacher Grade Entry Workflow

Teachers say that entering grades per student is slow and that it is hard to see which students remain ungraded. The Subject Grade Entry Page is updated to support **inline editing**, **bulk save**, and visual **counters** for graded/ungraded students. Rows with missing scores are highlighted.

### Iteration 5 – Payment Approval Feedback

Staff report that the Payment Approval Page does not clearly show the effect of approve/reject actions, and students are unsure of payment status after staff review. The design is updated to include **status badges**, **action history**, and clearer **confirmation messages**, and to show the **Paid Date** when a payment is approved.
